[SIZE=6]About Project[/SIZE]
dotOS Fan Edition based on dotOS and AOSP. We are providing cool features missing by dotOS Team. Our rom have lots of customizations and features. Stay with us! #FansTime

[SIZE=6]Features[/SIZE]
Our exclusive features:
- Android 12 (S) QS media player styles
- A lots of clock styles
- Privacy indicators
- Oneplus Launcher and few OnePlus apps

Few other cool features:
- Per app volume control
- Colored status bar
- Smart Charging and Smart Cutoff
- Few useful QS Tiles
- New permission request dialog design
